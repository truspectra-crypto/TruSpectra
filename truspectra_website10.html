About Us

Welcome to TruSpectra Testing and Research Laboratory Pvt. Ltd. – your trusted partner in scientific excellence and quality assurance. We are committed to delivering precise, reliable, and comprehensive contract testing services tailored to meet the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, cosmetic, food, and environmental sectors. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced analytical instrumentation, managed by a team of highly qualified professionals with extensive experience in regulatory compliance and analytical research.

At TruSpectra, we go beyond routine testing by focusing on accuracy, transparency, and innovation to support product safety, efficacy, and regulatory acceptance. We aim to be a strategic partner for our clients, assisting them in achieving the highest quality standards and ensuring their products meet both national and international regulatory requirements. Whether you are seeking method development, validation, stability studies, environmental monitoring, or water quality testing, TruSpectra delivers end-to-end solutions designed to meet your specific requirements.

Our mission is to empower industries with trusted analytical support, contributing to public health, environmental safety, and product excellence.

Our Services

Pharmaceutical Testing

Extensive analytical services to ensure pharmaceutical products meet the highest standards of safety, efficacy, purity, and regulatory compliance. This includes Raw Material Testing, Finished Product Testing, and Stability Studies.

Nutraceutical Testing

Advanced testing solutions for safe, high-quality dietary supplements and functional foods, including Nutritional Content Analysis, Contaminant & Adulteration Testing, and Shelf-Life & Stability Studies.

Cosmetic Product Testing

Ensuring safety and effectiveness of cosmetic and personal care products through Toxicological & Safety Testing and Performance & Claim Validation. Our state-of-the-art instrumentation and validated methodologies ensure timely, accurate, and reproducible results.
